Warehouse Reports

With this simplified Warehouse Reports, every individual will be able to create warehouse reports in a very less time without any cost/professional technical knowledge or help from anyone. The warehouse reports app by Ksolves provides your business the flexibility that is needed to make immediate changes so that you may always have the access to essential information on demand.

  • Filter functionality.
  • Stock Aging No Movement Report.

  • Stock Location Transfer Report.
  • No technical assistance needed.
  • Creation of multiple reports based on time and filter context.

User Guide

Frequently Asked Question

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise?
Yes, our app works with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community.
Need some customization in this app, whom to contact?
Please drop email at sales@ksolves.com or raise a ticket through Odoo store itself.
What if I set date between 01-03-2020 to 25-03-2020. Will it include 25-3-2020?
No, the app will provide report from 01-03-2020 00:00:00 to 25-03-2020 00:00:00. So select date between 01-03-2020 to 26-03-2020
Does the module provide stock valuation on the basis of Locations?
Yes, the app provides Stock valuation not only on the basis of Locations, but also for the Product Category, Warehouse, Company.
Does the module give daily reports for Inventory like per day sales, purchase, scrap?
Yes, the app provides reports on the basis of per day sales, purchase, scrap. Also, it shows the exhausted stock also..
I want a report for opening/closing stock from a particular duration?
This app provides reports for selected duration stock on basis of these 3 columns: Opening, Closing and onhand.
